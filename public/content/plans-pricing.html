Overview

Choosing the Right Version

Choose Solo if:

  • You work alone or with occasional subcontractors
  • You manage your own schedule and clients directly
  • You want a simple, powerful tool without team management overhead
  • You're an independent contractor, freelancer, or solopreneur

Choose Team if:

  • You have employees or dedicated crew members
  • You need to assign crews to jobs and manage schedules
  • You want timesheet approval workflows and performance reports
  • You need clients to book appointments directly via the AI phone system

Choose Enterprise if:

  • You have a large team across multiple locations
  • You need unlimited capacity for users, clients, and AI queries
  • You want SMS/MMS communication and a dedicated phone number
  • You need the lowest payment processing fees

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between Solo and Team?

Yes. You can upgrade from Solo to Team at any time from your account settings. Your data, clients, and history are preserved when you upgrade.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel at any time. Annual plans offer a discount but are also cancellable.

What happens if I reach my limit?

The system will notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan to increase your capacity at any time.

Do all team members get AI access?

Yes. Every user on a Team or Enterprise account has access to the In-App AI and WhatsApp AI assistant.

What is the Appointment Line callback vs AI booking?

In callback mode (Solo), clients call the appointment line and leave a callback request — you get notified and call them back. In AI booking mode (Team/Enterprise), the AI handles the entire conversation — it checks your availability, schedules the appointment, and confirms with the client, all without you needing to pick up the phone.
