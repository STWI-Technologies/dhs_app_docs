DHS IVR Appointment Line

Overview

The DHS Appointment Line is an AI-powered phone system that allows callers to find service providers, schedule appointments, manage existing bookings, and request callbacks — all by voice or keypad input. The system is fully bilingual, supporting both English and Spanish.

Phone Number: +1 (855) 244-4981

With the Appointment Line, callers can:

  • Search for service providers by name, extension code, or service type and location
  • Schedule new appointments with available providers
  • Check, change, or cancel upcoming appointments
  • Request a callback from a service provider
  • Get a company's direct phone number

How to Call

Step 1: Dial the Number

Call +1 (855) 244-4981 from any phone. The system will greet you and offer a language selection.

Step 2: Select Your Language

  • English: Press 1 or simply wait — the system defaults to English.
  • Spanish: Press 2 to continue in Spanish.

Step 3: Connect to a Provider

You can reach a service provider in several ways:

  • Automatic recognition — If you are a returning client, the system recognizes your phone number.
  • Extension code — Enter the 5-digit code your provider gave you.
  • Say the company name — Speak the name of the business you want to reach.
  • Press 9 — Search for a provider by service type and location.

Connecting to a Service Provider

Returning Clients

If you have called before or have an account with a service provider, the system may recognize your phone number automatically:

  • The system checks your caller ID against its records.
  • If you have an upcoming appointment, it will ask if you are calling about that appointment.
  • If your number is linked to multiple providers, the system will list them so you can choose which one you want to reach.

Extension Code

Your service provider may give you a 5-digit extension code (a number between 10000 and 99999). To use it:

  1. When prompted, enter the 5-digit code on your keypad.
  2. Press # (pound) to submit.
  3. The system will connect you directly to that provider's appointment line.

Extension codes are the fastest way to reach a specific provider on the shared line.

Say the Company Name

If you know the name of the company you want to reach, simply speak it when prompted. The system uses AI to match your spoken words to registered business names and connect you.

Search by Service and Location (Press 9)

If you do not have a specific provider in mind, press 9 to search by the type of service you need:

  1. Describe the service: Say what you need, such as "plumbing," "house cleaning," or "HVAC repair."
  2. Location detection: The system automatically detects your general location from your phone number's area code.
  3. Adjust location (optional): Say a different city and state, or a zip code, to search in another area. Press 2 to change location during a search.
  4. Browse results: Providers are listed sorted by distance, and include customer ratings when available. Press 0 to hear the next page of results.
  5. Select a provider: Press the number corresponding to the provider you want (1 through 8).

Tip: Say "any" to search across all service types in your area. Over 30 service categories are supported in both English and Spanish.

What You Can Do

Once connected to a service provider, the AI assistant can help you with any of the following:

Action Description
Schedule a new appointment Book a service visit by choosing an available date and time. The system will walk you through selecting a service, date, and time slot.
Check upcoming appointments Hear the details of your next scheduled appointment, including date, time, and service type.
Change an appointment Reschedule an existing appointment to a different date or time.
Cancel an appointment Cancel a scheduled appointment. The system will ask you to confirm before finalizing the cancellation.
Request a callback Ask the provider to call you back at a convenient time.
Get the company phone number Press 8 at any time to hear the provider's direct phone number.

Bilingual Support

The Appointment Line provides full bilingual support in English and Spanish. This includes:

  • All voice prompts are available in both languages.
  • AI conversation understands and responds in your selected language.
  • Service categories are recognized in both English and Spanish keywords.
  • Search results and appointment details are spoken in your chosen language.

For Service Providers

The Appointment Line is not just for callers — it is a powerful tool for service providers who want to offer their clients an easy way to book and manage appointments by phone.

Dedicated Phone Numbers

Service providers can request a dedicated phone number that connects callers directly to their business on the Appointment Line:

  • Callers who dial your dedicated number are connected to your business immediately — no extension code or search required.
  • You can record a custom greeting that callers hear when they connect.
  • Manage your greeting at any time: record a new one, listen to the current greeting, or reset it to the default.
  • Your dedicated number can be printed on business cards, flyers, and your website.

Extension Codes

Every service provider receives a unique 5-digit extension code (between 10000 and 99999) for use with the shared appointment line:

  • Give your extension code to clients so they can reach you quickly by entering it when they call +1 (855) 244-4981.
  • The code routes callers directly to your appointment system without needing to search or say your company name.
  • Extension codes are especially useful for repeat clients and can be included on invoices, emails, and marketing materials.

Tips for Callers

  • Speak naturally. The AI understands conversational language. You do not need to use specific keywords or commands — just describe what you need.
  • You can press keys or speak at any time. The system accepts both voice and keypad input throughout the call.
  • Press 0 to start over. If you get lost or want to begin again, press 0 at any point.
  • Say "any" to browse all services. When searching by service type, say "any" to see all available providers in your area regardless of category.
  • Have your information ready. For faster service, know the date and time you prefer before calling.
  • Check your caller ID. If you want to be recognized as a returning client, call from the phone number associated with your account.
