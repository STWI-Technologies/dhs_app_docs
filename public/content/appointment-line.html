DHS IVR Appointment Line

IVR stands for Interactive Voice Response — a phone number that answers calls with an automated menu.

Overview

The DHS Appointment Line is an AI-powered phone system that allows callers to find service providers, schedule appointments, manage existing bookings, and request callbacks — all by voice or keypad input. The system is fully bilingual, supporting both English and Spanish.

Phone Number: +1 (855) 244-4981

With the Appointment Line, callers can:

  • Connect to a service provider by extension code, company name, or service type and location
  • Schedule new appointments with available providers
  • Check, reschedule, or cancel upcoming appointments
  • Check a provider's available time slots
  • Request a callback from a service provider
  • Add or confirm a service address

How to Call

Step 1: Dial the Number

Call +1 (855) 244-4981 from any phone. The system will greet you and offer a language selection.

Step 2: Select Your Language

  • English: Press 1 or simply wait — the system defaults to English.
  • Spanish: Press 2, or say "español" to continue in Spanish.

If the system cannot detect your selection after three attempts, it will default to English automatically.

Step 3: Connect to a Provider

You can reach a service provider in several ways:

  • Automatic recognition — If you are a returning client, the system recognizes your phone number and connects you automatically.
  • Extension code — Enter the 5-digit code your provider gave you and press #.
  • Say the company name — Speak the name of the business you want to reach.
  • Press 9 — Search for a provider by service type and location.

Connecting to a Service Provider

Returning Clients

If you have called before or have an account with a service provider on the platform, the system recognizes your phone number automatically:

  • The system checks your caller ID against its records at the start of every call.
  • If you have an upcoming appointment, the system will greet you and ask if you are calling about that appointment.
  • If your number is linked to multiple providers, the system will list them so you can choose which one to reach.
  • Your caller ID is used to pre-fill your name and account details so the AI can assist you without needing to ask for your information again.

Extension Code

Every service provider on the platform has a unique 5-digit extension code (a number between 10000 and 99999). To use it:

  1. When prompted, enter the 5-digit code on your keypad.
  2. Press # (pound) to submit.
  3. The system will connect you directly to that provider's appointment line.

Extension codes are the fastest way to reach a specific provider. You can find your provider's code on their invoices, emails, or their website.

Say the Company Name

If you know the name of the company you want to reach, speak it when prompted. The system uses AI to match your spoken words to registered business names and connect you. If multiple matches are found, the system will list them and ask you to confirm.

Search by Service and Location (Press 9)

If you do not have a specific provider in mind, press 9 to search by the type of service you need:

  1. Describe the service: Say what you need, such as "plumbing," "house cleaning," or "HVAC repair." Say "any" to browse all available providers in your area.
  2. Location detection: The system automatically detects your approximate location from your phone number's area code.
  3. Adjust location (optional): Say a different city and state, or a zip code, to search in another area. Press 2 to change your location during a search.
  4. Browse results: Providers are listed and sorted by proximity. Press 0 to hear the next page of results.
  5. Select a provider: Press the number corresponding to the provider you want (1 through 8).

Note: Service discovery results come with a disclaimer that listed providers are independent operators. Over 30 service categories are supported in both English and Spanish.

What You Can Do

Once connected to a service provider, the AI assistant can help you with any of the following:

Action Description
Schedule a new appointment Book a service visit by choosing an available date and time. The AI will walk you through selecting a service, date, and time slot based on the provider's availability.
Check upcoming appointments Hear the details of your scheduled appointments, including date, time, and service type.
Reschedule an appointment Change an existing appointment to a different date or time.
Cancel an appointment Cancel a scheduled appointment. The system will ask you to confirm before finalizing the cancellation.
Check availability Ask about available time slots before booking, so you can choose a time that works for you.
Add a service address If your address is not on file, the AI can add it to your account during the call.
Request a callback Ask the provider to call you back. The system will record your name and phone number for the provider.

The AI conversation is handled by a voice assistant optimized for speed and clarity on phone calls. It understands natural language and does not require specific commands — just describe what you need.

Bilingual Support

The Appointment Line provides full bilingual support in English and Spanish throughout the entire call. This includes:

  • All voice prompts are available in both languages.
  • AI conversation understands and responds in your selected language.
  • Service categories are recognized in both English and Spanish when searching.
  • Appointment details and results are spoken in your chosen language.
  • Language can be selected by voice — say "español" to switch to Spanish without pressing any keys.

The Appointment Line is a powerful tool for service providers who want to give clients an easy way to book and manage appointments by phone, without managing inbound calls themselves.

Extension Codes

Every service provider receives a unique 5-digit extension code (between 10000 and 99999) for use with the shared appointment line:

  • Give your extension code to clients so they can reach you quickly when they call +1 (855) 244-4981.
  • The code routes callers directly to your appointment system without any searching.
  • Include it on invoices, estimate PDFs, emails, and marketing materials for repeat clients.

Custom Greeting Management (Dedicated Numbers)

If you have a dedicated phone number, you can manage your caller greeting at any time by calling your dedicated number from your registered phone:

  • Record a new greeting — personalize what callers hear when they connect.
  • Listen to your current greeting — hear what callers experience before they reach the menu.
  • Reset to the default greeting — remove your custom recording and return to the system default.

Greeting management is PIN-protected to ensure only authorized users can make changes.

Caller Identification & Client Matching

The system automatically recognizes callers who have an account linked to your business:

  • The caller's phone number is checked against your client list at the start of each call.
  • Recognized clients are greeted by name and connected to your appointment system without needing to identify themselves.
  • If a caller has an upcoming appointment with your business, the system surfaces it immediately so the caller can confirm, change, or cancel it quickly.
  • New callers are asked for their name before connecting, and this is recorded for your reference.

Business Context Awareness

The AI uses information from your service provider profile to provide relevant responses:

  • Service categories you offer inform what the AI discusses and offers to book.
  • Service area (city and state from your billing address) is used to confirm location relevance for callers.
  • Business hours / availability from your schedule are used to suggest appropriate time slots.

After-Hours Handling

The Appointment Line supports customizable messaging for calls received outside your configured business hours. Callers will hear a tailored after-hours greeting and can still request a callback so no lead is missed.

Emergency Transfer

When a caller uses urgent language, the system can offer a direct transfer to an on-call number for immediate assistance. This ensures time-sensitive calls are never left unanswered.

Tips for Callers

  • Speak naturally. The AI understands conversational language. You do not need to use specific commands — just describe what you need.
  • You can press keys or speak at any time. The system accepts both voice and keypad input throughout the call.
  • Press 0 to start over. If you get lost or want to begin again, press 0 at any point.
  • Say "any" to browse all services. When searching by service type, say "any" to see all available providers in your area regardless of category.
  • Have your information ready. For faster service, know the date and time you prefer before calling.
  • Call from your registered number. If you want to be recognized as a returning client, call from the phone number associated with your account. This skips the identification step and pre-loads your appointment history.
  • Say "goodbye" to end the call. You can end the AI conversation at any time by saying "goodbye" (or "adiós" in Spanish).
