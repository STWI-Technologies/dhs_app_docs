AI Assistant

Overview

The AI Assistant is your intelligent, voice-enabled helper built directly into the Direct Home Service app. It understands natural language in both English and Spanish, so you can simply type, speak, or send a photo to get things done — no menus to navigate, no forms to fill out.

The AI Assistant is available on both the web app and the mobile app (iOS and Android). It uses the same powerful AI engine as the DHS WhatsApp integration, but is accessed through a dedicated chat interface within the app itself.

Where to Find It

  • Web App — Look for the AI Assistant icon in the main navigation or sidebar.
  • Mobile App — Tap the AI Assistant button on the bottom navigation bar or home screen.

Supported Input Types

Input Type Description Availability
Text Type your request in plain English or Spanish Web & Mobile
Voice Record a voice message; the assistant transcribes and responds Web & Mobile
Image Upload or capture a photo for the assistant to analyze Web & Mobile

Getting Started

  1. Open the AI Assistant — Tap or click the AI Assistant icon from anywhere in the app.
  2. Choose your input method — Type a message, tap the microphone to record your voice, or tap the camera/upload icon to send a photo.
  3. Ask your question or give a command — Use natural, everyday language. For example: "Show me today's appointments" or "Create a new client named Maria Lopez."
  4. Review the response — The assistant will reply with the information you requested, confirm actions taken, or ask follow-up questions if needed.

There is no special syntax to learn. Just describe what you need the same way you would tell a coworker.

Voice Input

The AI Assistant supports voice recording on both the web and mobile apps. Instead of typing, simply tap the microphone button and speak your request.

  • Automatic transcription — Your voice message is converted to text instantly.
  • Bilingual support — Speak in English or Spanish; the assistant understands both.
  • Hands-free convenience — Ideal when you are in the field, driving, or have your hands full.

After recording, you will see the transcribed text in the chat. The assistant processes your request the same way it would a typed message.

Image Analysis

You can upload or capture photos and the AI Assistant will analyze them. This is useful in many field service scenarios:

  • Job site documentation — Take a photo of completed work and ask the assistant to describe it or attach it to a job.
  • Document reading — Photograph a handwritten note, a printed form, or a business card and ask the assistant to extract the information.
  • Equipment identification — Snap a picture of equipment or a product label and ask the assistant to identify it.
  • Issue reporting — Send a photo of a problem at a job site and ask for guidance or to log it in the system.

To send an image, tap the camera or upload icon in the chat interface, then select or capture your photo.

Bilingual Support

The AI Assistant provides full support for both English and Spanish. You do not need to change any settings — just communicate in whichever language you prefer.

  • Automatic language detection — The assistant detects whether you are writing or speaking in English or Spanish and responds in the same language.
  • Consistent responses — All information, confirmations, and follow-up questions are returned in your chosen language.
  • Mixed-language support — If you switch languages mid-conversation, the assistant adapts.

Tips & Example Conversations

The AI Assistant works best when you use natural, conversational language. Here are some tips and examples to help you get the most out of it.

General Tips

  • Be specific — Include names, dates, and job numbers when possible. "Show me John Smith's invoices" is better than "Show me invoices."
  • Use follow-ups — The assistant remembers your conversation context. After asking "Show me today's jobs," you can say "Which ones are incomplete?" without repeating yourself.
  • Ask for help — If you are unsure what the assistant can do, just ask: "What can you help me with?"
  • Combine requests — You can make complex requests like "Create a job for Maria Lopez at 123 Main St for next Monday at 9 AM and assign Crew A."

Security & Privacy

Your data security is a top priority. Here is how the AI Assistant keeps your information safe:

  • Authenticated access — All requests go through authenticated GraphQL APIs. The assistant can only access data if you are logged in with valid credentials.
  • Account-scoped data — The assistant only sees data belonging to your account. There is no cross-account data access.
  • No data sharing — Your queries and data are not shared with other accounts or used to train external models.
  • Role-based permissions — The assistant respects your user role. If you do not have permission to perform an action in the app, you cannot perform it through the assistant either.
  • Confirmation prompts — Destructive actions (like deleting records) always require explicit confirmation before they are executed.

Troubleshooting

Issue Solution
Assistant does not respond Check your internet connection and make sure you are logged in. Try refreshing the page or restarting the app.
Voice input not working Make sure you have granted microphone permissions to the app in your browser or device settings.
Image upload fails Ensure the image is under the maximum file size. Try a different image format (JPG or PNG).
"Daily limit reached" message You have used all your messages for the day. Limits reset at midnight, or you can upgrade your plan.
Assistant misunderstands request Try rephrasing your request with more specific details such as names, dates, or record numbers.
