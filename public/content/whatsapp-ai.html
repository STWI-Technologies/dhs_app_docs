WhatsApp AI Assistant

Overview

The WhatsApp AI Assistant is a conversational AI built right into WhatsApp that lets you manage your entire DHS business from your phone — using simple text messages or voice memos. Instead of switching between apps, you can search for clients, create appointments, send invoices, check your schedule, and much more, all from a WhatsApp chat.

With the WhatsApp AI Assistant you can:

  • Search and look up clients, jobs, estimates, invoices, crews, and more
  • Create new records like appointments, clients, jobs, estimates, and invoices
  • Update and manage existing records — change statuses, reschedule, edit fields
  • Track time — clock in, clock out, and review timesheets
  • Communicate — send messages and notifications to your team or clients
  • Get reports — daily briefings, schedule summaries, and business reports
  • Use utilities — check the weather, get directions, run quick calculations

The assistant supports over 103 tools and works in both English and Spanish. You can type your requests in natural, everyday language — no special commands to memorize.

Getting Started

Linking Your WhatsApp Number

Before you can use the WhatsApp AI Assistant, you need to link your WhatsApp phone number to your DHS account. Here is how:

  1. Open the DHS app on your phone or desktop.
  2. Go to Settings and select WhatsApp Integration.
  3. Enter the phone number associated with your WhatsApp account.
  4. Tap Link Number. You will receive a verification code via WhatsApp.
  5. Enter the verification code in the DHS app to confirm the link.
  6. Once verified, you can start messaging the DHS AI Assistant directly on WhatsApp.

Authentication and Security

Your WhatsApp AI session is protected by multiple layers of security:

  • Twilio Verification — Your phone number is verified through Twilio to confirm your identity.
  • Session Lock — After a period of inactivity, your session is automatically locked. To unlock it, you will receive a verification email with a secure link.
  • Email Verification — When your session locks, simply check your email, click the verification link, and your WhatsApp session will unlock automatically.

If someone else tries to message the assistant from an unlinked number, they will not be able to access any account data.

Voice Messages

You do not have to type everything out. The WhatsApp AI Assistant fully supports voice messages — just hold the microphone button in WhatsApp and speak your request.

  • Speak in English or Spanish — the system automatically detects and transcribes your language.
  • Natural speech — talk the way you normally would. For example, say "Schedule an appointment for Maria Lopez next Tuesday at nine in the morning" and it will understand.
  • Automatic processing — your voice memo is transcribed in real time, and the assistant responds as if you had typed the message.
  • Confirmation — the assistant will show you the transcription so you can verify it understood you correctly before taking action.

Voice messages are especially useful when you are on a job site and cannot type, or when you want to quickly dictate a note or update.

Sending Files and Images

The WhatsApp AI Assistant can handle files and images, making it easy to share documents and get AI-powered visual analysis.

Uploading Attachments

You can send files to the assistant just like you would send them to any WhatsApp contact:

  • Tap the attachment icon (paperclip) in WhatsApp.
  • Select the file you want to send (PDFs, documents, spreadsheets, etc.).
  • The assistant will process the file and let you know what it received.

Image Analysis

Send photos directly to the assistant for AI-powered analysis. This is useful for:

  • Job site photos — snap a picture of a property and ask the assistant to note conditions or create a job description.
  • Document scanning — photograph a handwritten note or printed document and have the assistant extract the information.
  • Issue documentation — send a photo of a problem area and the assistant can help you log it as a note on the relevant job or client record.

Downloading Files with Magic Links

When the assistant needs to send you a file (such as an estimate PDF, invoice, or report), it generates a secure magic link:

  • The link is unique to your session and expires after a set time for security.
  • Tap the link to download or view the file directly on your phone.
  • No login required — the link itself serves as your authentication.

Tips and Best Practices

Be Specific When You Can

The assistant understands natural language, but being specific helps it get things done faster:

  • Good: "Schedule an appointment for Maria Lopez at 123 Oak St next Tuesday at 9am for lawn mowing"
  • Also works: "I need to schedule something for Maria next week" (the assistant will ask follow-up questions)

Example Conversations

Starting your day:

  • You: "Good morning, give me my daily briefing"
  • Assistant: Provides today's schedule, open jobs count, pending invoices, and weather forecast.

Looking up a client before a visit:

  • You: "Show me everything about client Maria Lopez"
  • Assistant: Returns client details, properties, recent jobs, open estimates, and outstanding invoices.

Creating a job on the go:

  • You: "Create a new job for Maria Lopez — hedge trimming at her Main Street property"
  • Assistant: Creates the job and confirms the details. Asks if you want to assign a crew or schedule a date.

Clocking in for the day:

  • You: "Clock me in"
  • Assistant: Starts your time clock and confirms the timestamp.

Checking on payments:

  • You: "How many unpaid invoices do I have?"
  • Assistant: Shows a summary of outstanding invoices with total amount due.

Quick update from the field:

  • You: (voice memo) "Mark job 5678 as completed and add a note that the client was very happy with the work"
  • Assistant: Updates the job status and adds the note. Confirms both actions.

General Tips

  • Use voice messages when you are in the field and cannot type.
  • Ask for your daily briefing each morning to stay on top of your schedule.
  • Use the assistant for quick lookups instead of opening the full app — it is often faster.
  • Take advantage of image analysis — snap photos of job sites, receipts, or documents and let the AI process them.
  • Do not worry about exact wording — the assistant understands many different ways of asking for the same thing.
  • If the assistant gets something wrong, just correct it. Say "No, I meant..." and it will adjust.

Security

Your data security is a top priority. The WhatsApp AI Assistant uses multiple layers of protection to keep your business information safe.

Phone Number Verification

When you first link your WhatsApp number, it is verified through Twilio, a trusted communications platform. This ensures that only your verified phone number can access your DHS account through WhatsApp.

Session Lock and Unlock

To prevent unauthorized access, your WhatsApp AI session automatically locks after a period of inactivity. When your session is locked:

  1. The assistant will let you know your session has expired.
  2. A verification email is sent to the email address on your DHS account.
  3. Open the email and click the secure verification link.
  4. Your WhatsApp session unlocks automatically — you can continue right where you left off.

Magic Link Authentication

File downloads and sensitive actions use magic links — one-time-use URLs that are:

  • Unique to your session and request
  • Time-limited — they expire after a short period
  • Single-use — once clicked, the link cannot be reused

Data Privacy

  • Messages are processed securely and are not stored beyond what is needed to complete your request.
  • Voice messages are transcribed in real time and the audio is not retained.
  • The assistant only accesses data within your DHS account — it cannot see other users' data.
  • All communication between WhatsApp and the DHS system is encrypted.

Troubleshooting

Issue Solution
Assistant does not respond Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, your session may have expired — check your email for a verification link.
Session keeps locking This is normal security behavior after inactivity. Click the email verification link to unlock. Contact support if it happens too frequently.
Voice message not understood Try speaking more slowly and clearly. Reduce background noise. You can also type the request instead.
Wrong language detected Start your message with a clear phrase in your preferred language. The assistant will adjust.
Monthly limit reached Upgrade your plan under Settings > Subscription in the DHS app, or wait for the next billing cycle.
File download link expired Ask the assistant to generate a new download link for the file.
Cannot link WhatsApp number Make sure you are entering the same phone number registered with your WhatsApp account, including country code. Contact support if the issue persists.
