Settings & Configuration

Overview

The Settings section is your central hub for configuring every aspect of your DHS account. It controls your company information, user profile, availability, notifications, business rules, payment processing, and more.

To access Settings, navigate to Menu > Settings. The Settings page uses a sidebar + content layout: a navigation sidebar on the left lists all available sections, and the main content area on the right displays the selected section's form.

The Settings sidebar includes the following sections:

Section

Description

Account

Company information, client contact details, and billing address.

Profile

Your personal information, password, language, and profile picture.

Availability

Service availability schedule by day-of-week, exception dates, and seasonal exceptions.

Dashboard

Dashboard display preferences such as list limits and upcoming jobs window.

Notifications

Notification channel preferences (Email, SMS, Push, Desktop) per category.

Business

Counter settings, payment disclaimers, Zelle payment options, crew settings, privacy, and tax rates.

Timesheet Settings

Automatic timesheet linking configuration (account-level and personal override).

Accept Payments

Stripe Connect integration for accepting credit card payments from clients.


Note: Every settings section has a Save button and a Reset button. Changes are not saved until you click Save. The Reset button reverts all fields to their last saved values.

1.4 Sample Data

If your account was created with sample data (demo clients, jobs, etc.), a Sample Data section appears at the bottom of the Account page. This is only visible when sample data exists.

To delete all sample data:

  1. Click "Delete Sample Data" (red button).
  2. A confirmation modal appears with a warning about what will be deleted.
  3. Click "Delete Sample Data" in the modal to confirm.
  4. A progress bar tracks the deletion progress with a percentage indicator.
  5. Once complete, a success message appears and the section disappears.

Warning: Deleting sample data is permanent and cannot be undone. All sample clients, jobs, estimates, invoices, and related records will be removed.

Click "Save Account Info" to save your changes, or "Reset" to revert to the last saved values.

2.2 Change Password

To change your password:

  1. Enter your Current Password.
  2. Enter your New Password.
  3. Enter the new password again in Repeat Password to confirm.
  4. Click Save.

Password Requirements:

  • Minimum 10 characters.
  • Must contain at least one lowercase letter.
  • Must contain at least one uppercase letter.
  • Must contain at least one digit.
  • Must contain at least one special character (!@#$%^&*(),.?":{}|<>).
  • Must be different from your current password.

Each password field has a visibility toggle (eye icon) to show or hide the entered password.

3. Availability Settings

The Availability section controls when your business is available for scheduling. This affects which dates and times are available when creating appointments, jobs, and estimates.

Click Availability in the Settings sidebar to access it.

3.1 Weekly Schedule (Day/Time Slots)

Set your availability for each day of the week:

  • For each day (Monday through Sunday), you can define start time and end time.
  • You can add multiple time slots per day (e.g., morning and afternoon shifts).
  • Days without any time slots are considered unavailable.

3.2 Exception Dates

Add specific date ranges when your business is not available (e.g., holidays, vacations):

  • Click "Add Exception" to create a new exception.
  • Set the Start Date and End Date for the unavailable period.
  • Exception dates override your weekly schedule — even if a day is normally available, it will be blocked if it falls within an exception range.
  • Each exception appears as a card that can be edited or deleted.

3.3 Seasonal / Monthly Exceptions

For recurring unavailability patterns:

  • Monthly Exceptions: Toggle individual months (January through December) as unavailable. Useful for businesses that don't operate during certain months.
  • Seasonal Exceptions: Toggle entire seasons (Winter, Spring, Summer, Fall) as unavailable.
  • Each exception appears as a card that can be removed.

4. Dashboard Settings

6. Business Settings

The Business section configures operational rules for your service provider account. Click Business in the Settings sidebar to access it.

6.1 Counter Settings

Control the auto-numbering for estimates and invoices:

Setting

Description

Estimate ID Counter

The next number that will be assigned to a new estimate. Increase this if you want to start numbering from a specific point (e.g., set to 1001 to start estimates at #1001). Default: 1.

Invoice Number Counter

The next number that will be assigned to a new invoice. Works the same as the estimate counter. Default: 1.

6.2 Payment Disclaimer Settings

Configure the payment disclaimer text shown on invoices and estimates sent to clients:

  • Use Default Disclaimer (toggle):
  • When ON: A standard default disclaimer is used. A preview of the default text is shown below.
  • When OFF: A text area appears where you can write your own Custom Disclaimer Text.

6.3 Zelle Payment Details

If you accept Zelle payments, you can display Zelle payment instructions to your clients:

  • Show Zelle Payment Option (toggle):
  • When ON: A text area appears where you enter your Zelle Payment Option Text (e.g., your Zelle email or phone number and instructions). Maximum 500 characters with a live character counter.
  • When OFF: Zelle payment instructions are hidden from clients.

6.4 Crew Settings

Visibility: This section is only visible to users with Admin, Owner, or Support roles.

  • Allow Crew Time Log On Any Job (toggle):
  • When ON: Crew members can log time on any job, regardless of whether they are assigned to it.
  • When OFF: Crew members can only log time on jobs they are assigned to. An amber warning box appears explaining the restriction.

6.5 Privacy Settings

  • Do Not Share Information (toggle):
  • When ON: Your business information will not be shared with third parties. A blue info box confirms the privacy setting is active.
  • When OFF: Standard information sharing is enabled.

6.6 Tax Rates (Revenue Section)

Below the main business settings form, a separate Revenue section lets you manage tax rates. Tax rates are applied to services, products, estimates, invoices, and appointments.

  • Click "Add Tax Rate" to create a new tax rate. A modal appears with fields for:
  • Name — A label for the tax rate (e.g., "State Sales Tax", "County Tax").
  • Description — An optional description.
  • Rate (%) — The percentage (e.g., 8.25 for 8.25%).
  • Default — Mark one rate as the default that is automatically applied to new records.

  • Existing tax rates are displayed in a list showing name, description, and rate percentage.
  • Each tax rate can be edited or deleted.
  • Group tax rates are supported (a tax rate that combines multiple individual rates).

Click "Save Business Settings" to save the counter/disclaimer/Zelle/crew/privacy settings, or "Reset" to revert. Tax rates are saved independently through their own modals.

7. Timesheet Settings

The Timesheet Settings section controls how timesheet entries are automatically linked to jobs and appointments. Click Timesheet Settings in the Settings sidebar to access it.

7.1 Account-Level Setting

Visibility: This section is only visible to users with Admin or Owner roles.

  • Enable Automatic Linking (toggle):
  • When ON: When crew members start a timer on a job or appointment, the resulting timesheet entry is automatically linked to that record.
  • When OFF: Timesheet entries are created without automatic linking. An amber warning box explains the implications.

7.2 Your Timesheet Preference (Per-User Override)

Individual users can override the account-level setting for their own timesheet behavior:

  • Disable Automatic Linking For Me (toggle):
  • When ON: Automatic linking is disabled for your timesheet entries only, even if the account-level setting is enabled.
  • When OFF (or not set): The account-level default applies.
  • This toggle is disabled (grayed out) when the account-level automatic linking is already turned off, since there is nothing to override.

7.3 How Automatic Linking Works

A "How It Works" section at the bottom explains the 3-step process:

  1. A crew member starts a timer on a job or appointment.
  2. When the timer stops, a timesheet entry is created.
  3. The entry is automatically linked to the source job/appointment.

Click "Save Timesheet Settings" to apply changes.

9. Navigating Between Settings Sections

Use the sidebar navigation on the left to switch between sections. Each section has:

  • An icon identifying the section type.
  • A title (e.g., "Account", "Business").
  • A brief description of what the section manages.

The currently active section is highlighted in the sidebar. Each section has its own URL (e.g., /settings-business, /settings-notifications), so you can bookmark specific settings pages.

The content header always shows:

  • The main title "Settings" with the subtitle "Manage service provider settings".
  • Below that, the current section title and its description.
