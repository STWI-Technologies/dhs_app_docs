Appointments

Organize site visits before the work begins. Schedule, track, and transition seamlessly.

Want to See It In Action?

What You Can Do:

  • Manage pre-job visits like evaluations, inspections, and walkthroughs
  • Create appointments with client, property, crew, and service details
  • Track status visually: Draft, Scheduled, En Route, Started, Completed, Canceled, No Show
  • Start timers that auto-update appointment status (En Route or Started)
  • Mark appointments as Priority or Callback follow-ups
  • Communicate with clients via chat or keep internal notes
  • Attach files and documents for easy reference
  • Log visit time manually for work, travel, office, or job prep
  • Add services with pricing, taxes, and discounts
  • View Related Records: linked jobs, estimates, and invoices
  • Convert completed appointments to Jobs, Estimates, or Invoices
  • Search by appointment name, client, or crew
  • Filter by status to focus on what matters

Tips for Success

  • Use the Priority toggle for urgent pre-job visits
  • Enable Override to schedule outside standard hours
  • Use Start Timer to automatically track time and update status
  • Convert completed appointments to jobs or estimates to streamline workflow
  • Link appointments as Callbacks to maintain job history and continuity
