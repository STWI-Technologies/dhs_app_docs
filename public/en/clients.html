Clients

Manage customer info. Track properties. Build lasting relationships.

Want to See It In Action?

What you can do:

  • Add residential or business clients
  • Store multiple property addresses per client
  • View all estimates, jobs, and invoices linked to each client
  • Chat with clients directly through the Inbox
  • Attach notes and documents for future reference
  • Search and filter your client list quickly and efficiently

Smart Search & Filtering

  • Quick Search by name, email, or address
  • Advanced Filters: Narrow by city and state
  • Easily switch views between All, Residential, Business, Archived

Tips for success

  • Use one profile to manage multiple properties for a single client
  • Keep contracts and photos in the attachments tab
  • Archive inactive clients for cleaner dashboards
  • All actions (estimates, jobs, invoices) can be initiated from the client profile
