Users

Manage your team. Control access. Stay organized.

Want to See It In Action?

What you can do:

  • Add new team members with custom roles
  • Assign roles and permissions: Admin, Support, or Crew
  • Enable/disable user access as needed
  • Search and filter users quickly
  • Update contact info and roles anytime

Tips for success

  • Only Admins can manage user permissions
  • Use color-coded roles for fast visual recognition
  • Users added here can also be assigned to Crews for scheduling
  • You can't delete users, but disabling keeps your records intact
